Methodology

Priority Score = Pot. Incr Rev (Mar–Dec 2026) × Action Confidence × Google ROAS Index

Action Confidence weights how strongly we believe Meta can deliver. Google ROAS Index is a quality lens (capped 0.5–1.2×) — strong Google ROAS gives a +20% uplift, weak gives a discount.
